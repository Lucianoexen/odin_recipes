Recipe Meat Empanadas

Ingredients

  • 12 pastry dough
  • 500gr grinded meat
  • 3 onions
  • 2 colorful bellpeppers

Steps

  1. Prepare the filling, cooking the meat first and adding secondly finely chopped onion and bellpepper
  2. Let it rest until and cold (!Avoid leaving more than 2 hours outside)
  3. Fill the pre-bought dough, folding it to create the half-moon shape
  4. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C),bake for 20-25 minutes or until they are golden brown.
