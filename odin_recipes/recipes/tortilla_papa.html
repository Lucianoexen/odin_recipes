Potato Omelette Recipe

Ingredients

  • 500gr potatoes
  • 300gr onions
  • 5 whole eggs

Steps

  1. Clean all the potatoes and peel them
  2. Cut both potatoes and onions in slices, potato thinner than the onion
  3. Fry the potatoes, and caramelize the onions
  4. Mix everything into a bowl and add the eggs
  5. In a low heat pan, combine everything with a lid on and flip for each side
